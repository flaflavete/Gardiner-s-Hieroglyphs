Gardiner Hieroglyphs

Would you like a quick guide on how to use this tool?

𓏟

Browse by Category

Use the left sidebar to navigate the 26 Gardiner sign categories — from A (Man) to Aa (Unclassified). You can also use the search bar at the top to find signs by number, phoneme or description.

𓏟

Click a Sign

Click any sign card to add it to your word. Signs with a red phoneme tag contribute to the transliteration automatically. Determinatives (no phoneme) are added silently.

𓏟

Multiple Phonemes

Some signs have more than one phonetic value. When you click them, a popup appears — choose the phoneme you need, or select "no phoneme" to use the sign as a determinative.

nfr

Auto-Transliteration

The right panel builds the transliteration automatically as you add signs. Edit it freely below — use the special character buttons (ꜣ ꜥ ḥ ḫ ẖ š ṯ ḏ…) to insert Egyptological characters.

𓏟

Copy & Paste

Use the Copy buttons to copy the hieroglyphs, the transliteration, or both together — ready to paste into Word, Pages or any text editor.

Click a sign in the word display to remove it.
